# Privacy Policy

**App Name:** BMI Calculator

## Introduction
BMI Calculator is a free application developed by Baburam Nabik. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

## Information Collection and Use
BMI Calculator does not collect, store, use, or share any personal information from users. The app only calculates Body Mass Index (BMI) based on the height and weight entered by the user.

All calculations are performed locally on the user's device. No user data is transmitted to any server or stored by the developer.

## Third-Party Services
This app does not use third-party services that collect personal data.

## Data Security
Since BMI Calculator does not collect or store any personal information, no personal data is transmitted or stored.

## Children's Privacy
BMI Calculator does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

## Data Retention and Deletion
BMI Calculator does not retain any user data. All calculations occur locally on the device and no information is stored by the developer.
